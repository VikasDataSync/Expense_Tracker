Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information you provide directly to us. This includes your account information (email and password), expense entries (amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions), and any other data you choose to enter into the application.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide solely for the purpose of operating and improving the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries and insights about your spending patterns, and maintaining the functionality of the application.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may use trusted third-party services for hosting and infrastructure purposes, but these providers are contractually obligated to keep your data confidential and use it only for the services they provide to us.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data at any time. You can export your expense data or delete your account through the application settings. Upon account deletion, all your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us. We are committed to addressing any concerns you may have about your privacy.
